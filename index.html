TOOLKIT OVERVIEW

The toolkit builds on experience from a pilot project HRB4D in Zambia, implemented by DIHR, Kukula Capital and six local Zambian partner businesses. The pilot resulted in a number of social interventions to improve the living conditions of workers and their dependents, including the installation of rainwater harvesting units, medication scheme, purchase of bicycles and water storage solution for employee households
